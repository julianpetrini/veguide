Private Chef Experience

We offer private chef experiences catered by talented local chefs to make any corporate gathering, team building activity, rehearsal dinner, or celebration a truly memorable affair. We will provide you and your guests with a memorable and special dining experience.


Exclusive. Vegetarian. Dining.

It's not often you get to interact with a talented local chef while they prepare an exquisite menu for you. Our chefs have worked in some of the finest restaurants, and now they are sharing their skills on a more personal level. Throughout your event, our dedicated chefs will prepare a custom menu tailored to match your vision. If you'd like to have an interactive experience, we can arrange chef demos, demonstrations, tastings, and interactive table games. Whether you’re looking to dine and entertain in your own home, or in one of our beautiful restaurants, we have created a dining experience that your guests definitely wont forgetting any time soon.
